History

25 Years of Service

Allred’s Restaurant, perched at 10,551 feet above Telluride, offers an unforgettable dining experience with breathtaking mountain views. Since 2000, we’ve been the town’s premier destination for elevated cuisine, exceptional service, and rustic elegance. Built on the vision of transforming Telluride into a world-class destination, Allred’s blends locally inspired flavors with a warm, inviting atmosphere. Join us for a meal above the rest.
